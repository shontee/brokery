About Us

The Brokery Group

As a pioneer and innovator of the retail foreign exchange industry. Brokery Group has long established itself as the provider of online trading services in the Foreign exchange market, serving clients since 1994.

Brokery financial platform provides professional services to our clients with a wide range of services including dealing in Index Futures, Gold Futures, Crude Oil Futures, Forex and etc. Brokery involves in every development process with our comprehensive skill from the initial product implementation, continuous technical support and functional security in the indices, futures and foreign exchange to operation and transactions.

To satisfy the needs of each and every client's specific requirements, Brokery strives to provide reliable, professional services with precision. In Brokery, we expand our knowledge and services to assist our client to transact successfully in the most effective and reliable trading environment, as to meet the needs and satisfaction of our clients.
